About Us

We are Ready to Produce High Quality Eggs

Maduna Happy Hen eggs is a local farm situated in Umlazi and proudly owned by co-founder Tomas Maduna. Our primary focus is on providing high-quality eggs to the community. Conveniently located, our farm offers easy accessibility to residents throughout the Durban area. Our commitment to quality ensures that the eggs produced at Maduna Happy Hen Eggs meet rigorous standards. With a passion for farming and a dedication to excellence, Tomas Maduna oversees the operations to guarantee the freshness and nutritional value of our eggs.

Choose Maduna Happy Hen Eggs for a reliable and convenient source of high-quality eggs, produced with care and a commitment to the community.
